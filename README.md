## üéØ Business Problem

While revenue was growing steadily, profitability showed signs of
pressure due to:

-   Aggressive regional discounting\
-   Rising shipping costs\
-   Uneven margin distribution across regions\
-   Product-level profitability imbalance

The dashboard was designed to identify root causes and recommend
data-driven corrective actions.
